Maasai Mara Wilderness Expedition

Duration: 4 Days / 3 Nights

Highlights:
Game Drives: Experience the thrill of wildlife spotting with daily game drives in the iconic Maasai Mara National Reserve.
Camping Under the Stars: Stay in comfortable tents under the African sky, immersing yourself in the sounds of the wilderness.
Cultural Encounters: Interact with the Maasai people, learning about their traditions, dances, and way of life.
Transportation: Round-trip transportation from Nairobi in a comfortable safari vehicle.
Price: Starting from $699 per person

Mount Kenya Trek & Safari

Duration: 6 Days / 5 Nights

Highlights:
Mountain Trekking: Embark on a trek to the summit of Mount Kenya, Africa's second-highest peak.
Wildlife Safari: Explore the nearby Ol Pejeta Conservancy for a chance to see the Big Five.
Camping Adventure: Camp at scenic locations during the trek, surrounded by breathtaking landscapes.
Transportation: Airport transfers and all transportation during the safari included.
Price: Starting from $1,199 per person

Coastal Paradise Retreat

Duration: 7 Days / 6 Nights

Highlights:
Diani Beach Relaxation: Unwind on the pristine beaches of Diani, enjoying the turquoise waters and white sands.
Camping by the Ocean: Experience beachfront camping for a unique coastal adventure.
Snorkeling Excursion: Explore the vibrant underwater world with a guided snorkeling tour.
Transportation: Comfortable transfers from Mombasa to Diani and back.
Price: Starting from $899 per person
